About Me

I’m Macallan Butler, a graduate of Arizona State University with a degree in Graphic Information Technology, specializing in full-stack web design. With a passion for UX and SEO, I aim to combine creativity and technical expertise to craft impactful digital experiences.

Originally from Chicago, I’m a 24-year-old driven by adaptability, loyalty, and a love of exploration. My ultimate professional goal is to establish my own web design and SEO business, empowering small businesses around the world to thrive in the digital space.

One project I’m particularly proud of is my interactive quiz website focused on snow leopard conservation—a project that reflects my commitment to creating meaningful and engaging online experiences, though I recognize there’s always room for improvement.

When I’m not designing or optimizing, I enjoy lifting weights, following the thrilling world of Formula 1, and immersing myself in a good book. Whether collaborating on innovative designs or building SEO strategies, I’m always eager to learn, grow, and make a lasting impact.
